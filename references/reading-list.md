# Reading List: Narrative-Driven GTM Alignment

Curated research, articles, and case studies on using shared narrative as an alignment mechanism in go-to-market organizations.

---

## Foundational Research

| Source | Title | Why it matters |
|--------|-------|----------------|
| HBR | [Your Strategy Needs a Story](https://hbr.org/2023/02/your-strategy-needs-a-story) | 70% of transformations fail from lack of alignment. Stories turn strategy into shared mental models. |
| HBR | [How to Build a Strategic Narrative](https://hbr.org/2016/03/how-to-build-a-strategic-narrative) | The only way to get strategic alignment is with a simple story understood in five minutes or less. |
| HBR | [Why Cross-Functional Collaboration Stalls](https://hbr.org/2024/06/why-cross-functional-collaboration-stalls-and-how-to-fix-it) | 78% of leaders report "collaboration drag." More meetings is not the answer. |
| McKinsey | [The CEO's Role as Chief Storyteller](https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/strategy-and-corporate-finance/our-insights/the-ceos-role-as-chief-storyteller) | Leaders who treat narrative as strategy more than double the odds of success. |
| Forrester | [The Truth About B2B Sales and Marketing Alignment](https://www.forrester.com/blogs/the-truth-about-b2b-sales-and-marketing-alignment/) | 82% of execs think they're aligned. 65% of practitioners say they're not. |
| Gartner | [2025 Trends for Product Marketing Leaders](https://www.gartner.com/en/articles/2025-trends-for-product-marketing-leaders) | 50% of PMM leaders cite lack of cross-functional collaboration as top barrier. |
| Gartner | [Sales and Marketing Alignment](https://www.gartner.com/en/articles/sales-and-marketing-alignment) | Aligned GTM orgs grow 19% faster and are 15% more profitable. |

## Practitioner Frameworks

| Author | Resource | Key idea |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Andy Raskin | [The Greatest Sales Deck I've Ever Seen](https://medium.com/the-mission/the-greatest-sales-deck-ive-ever-seen-4f4ef3391ba0) | Five-element narrative framework: name the change, winners/losers, promised land, magic gifts, evidence. |
| Andy Raskin | [The Power of Strategic Narrative (Lenny's Podcast)](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/the-power-of-strategic-narrative) | How Gong, Zuora, and others use strategic narrative as a company operating system. |
| April Dunford | [Obviously Awesome / Positioning](https://www.aprildunford.com/) | Positioning starts with competitive alternatives, not your product. Narrative follows positioning. |
| Richard King | [Why Smart PMMs Never Ask for Stakeholder Alignment](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/why-smart-pmms-never-ask-stakeholder-alignment-richard-king-r001e/) | You don't ask for alignment. You design for it. |
| Richard King | [The Core Skills for Product Marketing in 2030](https://productmarketingalliance.substack.com/p/the-core-skills-for-product-marketing) | Cross-functional alignment is a permanent core PMM skill. Gaps between functions will widen. |
| Dave Steer (GitLab) | [GTM Framework AMA](https://sharebird.com/h/product-marketing/ama/gitlab-vice-president-of-product-marketing-dave-steer-on-go-to-market-strategy) | Develop your GTM framework as a narrative. It creates clarity of thought and makes socialization easier. |

## Case Studies

| Company | Source | What happened |
|---------|--------|---------------|
| Zuora | [Best Sales Deck Ever](https://www.zuora.com/resource/best-sales-deck-ever/) | Coined "subscription economy." Built entire company narrative around a market shift. Used for 10+ years across sales, marketing, product, recruiting, and IPO. |
| Drift | [Category Creation Playbook](https://profitsnack.com/p/drift-conversational-marketing-playbook) | Named a shift ("buyers want conversations, not forms"), coined "conversational marketing," evangelized the term, let competitors use it to validate the category. |
| Gong | [What is Revenue Intelligence?](https://www.gong.io/blog/what-is-revenue-intelligence) | Created the "revenue intelligence" category. Narrative drives product, marketing, sales, and hiring decisions. |
| MuleSoft | [GTM Foundations: 20 Employees to $6.5B Acquisition](https://thegtmengineer.substack.com/p/the-gtm-foundations-taking-mulesoft) | Stage 2 pipeline as unifying metric. Cultural alignment and outcomes-first hiring created cohesion across functions. |
| Redgate | [April Dunford positioning example](https://www.aprildunford.com/) | Repositioned database tools around "Database DevOps." Same products, new narrative. Inbound leads surged. |
| iMocha | [How We Crafted iMocha's Strategic Narrative](https://thecmojournal.substack.com/p/how-we-crafted-imochas-strategic) | Step-by-step documentation of applying the Raskin framework at a growth-stage company. |
