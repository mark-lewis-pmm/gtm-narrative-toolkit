# Shared Narrative Canvas

A one-page framework for building the story that aligns your entire GTM org. If every function can answer these five questions roughly the same way without checking a document, you have alignment.

---

## The Five Questions

### 1. Why is the market shifting right now?

What is the big, undeniable change in the world that creates urgency? This is not about your product. It's about your customer's world.

## How to use this

1. Draft it alone first. Don't start with a committee.
2. Test it with 2-3 sales reps. If they can't retell it after one read, simplify.
3. Bring it to your leadership team. Use it to surface disagreements, not to paper over them.
4. Refresh it every quarter. If the story from Q4 still works in Q2, you're either lucky or not paying attention.

## What this is not

This is not a messaging doc. It's not a positioning statement. It's not a sales pitch.

It's the upstream story that all of those things draw from. When this is clear, messaging docs write themselves. When it's not, no amount of wordsmithing will fix your alignment problem.
